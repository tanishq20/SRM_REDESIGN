AT SRM, WE DON'T JUST TEACH

We provide an inspiring environment through freedom, creativity and innovation.

  • SRMIST offers a challenging, meaningful work in an open environment that celebrates diversity in all its forms. Being part of SRMIST is more than just a career – it could be a life-changing experience.
  • At SRMIST everyone has the right and opportunity to develop and see through their ideas. No matter what you do, it is an amazing place to be.
  • Our academics show the way with our professional, administrative and support staff creating a world-class working environment and enable them to continue achieving internationally renowned excellence in research and teaching.
  • We offer market-competitive rewards package that consider your needs at different life stages and also provide a supportive environment that allows you to enjoy a healthy work-life balance.

  • General Requirements
    • Candidates should have an excellent academic record, good communication skills and a demonstrated ability to carry out original and creative research
    • Candidates with Ph.D. or those who have submitted their Doctoral thesis would be preferred. However, to promote excellence and diversity, candidates with minimum qualification in inter-disciplinary areas would also be considered for selection
    • Individuals who have demonstrated exceptional abilities in the practice of engineering and technology in public or private sector organizations/industry at senior leadership positions but not having a Ph.D. degree, may also be considered for appropriate positions
  • Instructions to Candidates
    • Faculty positions are available in all departments at all levels
    • Candidates should possess good health. If selected, they may be required to undergo a medical examination and satisfy such medical authority as SRM may prescribe
    • Foreign nationals can also apply for appointments but it would be limited to a fixed period of contractual appointment
    • Candidates from outstation would be provided accommodation on need basis a day earlier/later to the Interview date but no TA/DA will be provided for attending the Interview
    • Internal candidates of SRMIST need to take the print of the duly filled application and should submit to the HR Department duly forwarded by Dean/HOD
  • Salary Details
    • As per 7th Pay Commission under SRMIST Norms
    • Salary would not be a constraint for deserving candidates
  • Benefits Other Than Salary
    • Seed Grant for Research
    • Group Accident Insurance as per University rules
    • Financial Support through Professional Development Allowance (PDA) for attending Conferences, Workshops, etc., as per University Rules
    • Coverage of EPF as per rules
    • Free Transportation (to & fro) from Campus to City & vice versa
    • Fee Concession for Children in SRM CBSE School
    • Concessional Medical Facility
  • Filling The Application Form
    • Please use Google Chrome/Mozilla Firefox browser to fill the form to ensure proper data capture
    • Only ONLINE applications will be accepted
    • Most forms are self-explanatory
    • Ensure that you meet the required eligibility, as on date of submission of the application
    • Please note that to fill the main application form, you are required to REGISTER, providing your User Name, Email-id, Password (to be saved for your future use), first and last name. Usage of at least one special character while filling up the form is mandatory
    • After registration, a mail with confirmation link will be sent to the Email-id registered. Click on the link which will open the Login Page where the candidates shall use their registered Email-id and Password to fill the application form
    • Altogether, there are 7 Sections – Application Details, Personal Details, Qualification Details, Experience Details, Productivity Details, Reference Details and Document Upload. Complete all mandatory fields (* – asterisk marked) and then SAVE your application at the end of every section
    • You shall EDIT/ SAVE/ LOGOUT in between Sections until the Portal is ON LIVE. However, after submission (after completing all the 7 Sections filled), you shall edit within 24 hours of time from the time of submission. Else, the data captured earlier would be deemed to be final
    • Photo upload is mandatory (Max. size: 1 MB) in jpg format
    • SUBMIT the form after completing all sections

    NOTE: After submission, a PDF generated Application Form will be sent to your Email-id registered. Candidates shall send the self-attested copies of the Experience Certificates, Educational Qualifications, Three best Journal Publications, Papers and other documents, as mentioned in the Application Form or wherever desired, with the printed and signed Application.

  • Selection Procedure
    • All applications will be scrutinized by the selection committee to determine the suitability
    • Suitable candidates will have Skype Interview before a panel of experts at the initial stage
    • Suitable candidates will be called in person to assess their Teaching, Research and other academic capabilities
    • Candidates are required to carry the original of all documents along with the application form (duly signed) at the time of personal interview for verification
    • Selected candidates will be informed through Email/Post
    • The decision of SRMIST will be final and binding
  • Important Notes & Exceptions
    • Canvassing in any form will be a disqualification
    • Mere fulfilment of minimum qualifications and experience does not entitle a candidate to be called for interview or his/her selection
    • SRM reserves the right to not fill positions in any of the above and/or any other specialization(s) without assigning any reason
    • Candidates called for interview to fill a vacancy may be considered for lower/higher post, at the discretion of the committee
    • Depending on the qualification and experience, deserving candidates may be offered a salary at a higher level irrespective of the cadre
    • Providing false information, misrepresentation of facts, plagiarism, etc., will disqualify the candidate’s application/ appointment at any point of time
    • All rights are reserved with SRM, under the Jurisdiction of the High Court of Madras, Tamilnadu

    Notification for Recruitment

    Applications are invited from eligible candidates for the following posts in the prescribed form available / downloadable from the career portal of SRM Institute of Science and Technology. The duly completed application forms should be signed, scanned and mailed to dean.agri@srmist.edu.in on or before 15 November 2019.

    PROFESSORS/ ASSOCIATE PROFESSORS in Agroecology, Horticulture

    ASSISTANT PROFESSORSin Agroforestry, Agricultural Engineering, Agricultural Extension, Horticulture (Vegetable Science), Horticulture (Spices and Plantation Crops), Information Technology, Genetics and Plant Breeding, Plant Pathology, Soil Science

    TUTORS in Agroforestry, Horticulture, Horticulture (Spices and Plantation Crops), Information Technology, Genetics and Plant Breeding, Agricultural Engineering, Soil Science.

    Essential Qualifications

    Professor

    i. A Candidate with Ph.D. qualification in the concerned / relevant discipline and published work of high quality, with a minimum of 10 publications as books and/or research/policy papers, of which at least five papers in refereed journals in the previous cadre.
    i. A Master’s degree in the relevant discipline with at least 55% marks or equivalent (or an equivalent grade in point scale wherever grading system is followed).
    ii. A minimum of ten years of experience in teaching/ research/ extension in university/college or accredited Research Institutions including experience of guiding candidates for research, in the cadre of Assistant Professor and above.
    iii. At least two years of experience in the Associate Professor cadre or equivalent.

    Note: Specialization for the post of Professor (Agroecology) : Agronomy / Soil Science & Agricultural Chemistry / Agricultural Microbiology with thesis/research experience in agroecology / organic farming / sustainable agriculture /soil microbiology/ bio fertilizers / soil organic matter will be considered.

    Associate Professor

    i. A candidate with a Ph.D. Degree in the concerned / relevant discipline.
    ii. A Master’s degree in the relevant discipline with at least 55% marks or equivalent (or an equivalent grade in point scale wherever grading system is followed).
    iii. A minimum of five publications as books or research/policy papers of which at least three papers in refereed journals in the cadre of Assistant Professor.
    iv. A minimum of eight years of experience in teaching/research/ extension in the cadre of Assistant Professor in teaching/research/extension in University / College or accredited Research Institution in the cadre of Assistant Professor.

    Note: Specialization for the post of Associate Professor (Agroecology): Agronomy / Soil Science & Agricultural Chemistry / Agricultural Microbiology with thesis/research experience in agroecology / organic farming / sustainable agriculture /soil microbiology/ bio fertilizers / soil organic matter will be given preference.

    Assistant Professor

    i. A candidate with a Ph.D. Degree in the concerned / relevant discipline.
    ii. A Master’s degree in the relevant discipline with at least 55% marks or equivalent (or an equivalent grade in point scale wherever grading system is followed).
    iii. Experience in teaching / research will be preferred

    Tutors

    i. PG in concerned specialization.
    ii.A Master’s degree in the relevant discipline with at least 55% marks or equivalent (or an equivalent grade in point scale wherever grading system is followed).
    iii.Experience in teaching will be an added advantage
